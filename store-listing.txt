<en-US>
Tesla doesn't support Android Auto — Castla is the solution. Extend your Android phone screen to Tesla's built-in browser in real-time. No extra hardware, no cables needed.

Use Google Maps, Waze, or any navigation app on Tesla's big screen. Stream Spotify, YouTube Music, and more with audio playing through Tesla.

WITH PRO SPLIT VIEW
Run two apps side by side on Tesla's display. Navigation on the left, YouTube or Netflix on the right — perfect for passengers to enjoy entertainment while the driver navigates. One screen, two apps, zero compromise.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Connect your phone and Tesla to the same WiFi (or use phone hotspot)
2. Tap "Start Mirroring" in the app
3. Open the URL in Tesla's browser
4. Enter the 4-digit PIN — done!

FEATURES
• Real-time screen mirroring with H.264 hardware encoding
• Touch control — operate your phone directly from Tesla's touchscreen
• Audio streaming to Tesla (Android 10+)
• Built-in browser for DRM content (YouTube, Netflix)
• Split View — two apps side by side on Tesla
• Virtual Display via Shizuku — apps run independently without showing on your phone
• Bluetooth Tesla auto-detection with one-tap start
• PIN & QR code secure connection
• Home screen widget
• 9 languages supported

FREE vs PRO
Free: 720p / 30fps, full features. Watch 1 short ad = 10 free sessions.
PRO (one-time purchase): 1080p / 60fps, Split View, all apps unlocked, ad-free.

REQUIREMENTS
• Android 8.0+
• Tesla with web browser (Model S/3/X/Y)
• Same WiFi or phone hotspot
</en-US>
